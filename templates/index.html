Noctilucent cloud forecast

Welcome to the Noctilucent Cloud Forecast. Currently this forecast is not real time but generated for the past summer. The maps display the likelyhoods of Noctilucent clouds for Finland for the twilight hours by hour, in the timeframe when the clouds have the possibility to appear.

What are noctilucent clouds?

Noctilucent clouds are high altidute clouds that most commonly but rarely appear during summer twilight producing a beautiful light effect by reflecting sunlight that has already gone below the horizon.
They are one of the few sky phenomenan viewable during the Finnish summer.
