What are noctilucent clouds?

Noctilucent clouds are high altidute clouds that most commonly but rarely appear during summer twilight producing a beautiful light effect by reflecting sunlight that has already gone below the horizon.
They are one of the few sky phenomenan viewable during the Finnish summer.
